About Akshay Medicals

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Preetipal Malgi, Akshay Medicals has been serving the community of Sirsi with dedication and care. What started as a small pharmacy has grown into a trusted healthcare destination, known for our quality medicines and personalized service.

Located conveniently opposite Government Hospital on Church Road, we've been the first choice for many families in Sirsi for their healthcare needs.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare products and services to our community. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers through trust, care, and professional service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver medicines?

Yes, we offer free delivery within Sirsi city limits for orders above ₹500. For emergency medicines, we provide immediate delivery regardless of order value.

Can I get advice on medication?

Absolutely! Our licensed pharmacists are available during store hours to provide expert advice on medications, side effects, and proper usage.

Do you stock specialty medicines?

We maintain a wide range of specialty medicines. If we don't have something in stock, we can usually arrange it within 24-48 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and mobile wallets for your convenience.
